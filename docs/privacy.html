TwinLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

TwinLens is designed as a camera utility that helps you record with front and rear cameras. We keep the app simple and privacy-conscious.

Information We Collect

TwinLens does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information. Photos and videos you capture are stored on your device or in your Photos library when you choose to save them.

Camera, Microphone, and Photos Access

TwinLens requests camera and microphone access so it can preview and record media. TwinLens may request Photos access when you save captures to your Photos library. These permissions are used only for the features you choose to use.

Media Handling

Your captures are processed locally on your device. TwinLens does not upload your photos, videos, or audio recordings to our servers.

Purchases

If you buy TwinLens Pro, the purchase is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment details.

Analytics and Tracking

TwinLens does not use third-party advertising trackers. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated to explain what is collected and why.

Children's Privacy

TwinLens is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
